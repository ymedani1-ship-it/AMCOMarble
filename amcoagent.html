Our Story

Built on Integrity,
Defined by Quality

Founded by A.M. Salama and Dr. A. Medani, AMCO Marble is a division of the Alexandria Maritime Consultative Office. We produce and supply a vast variety of natural stones and decorative tiles for building construction — from rough blocks and slabs to precision-cut tiles.

We offer the finest marble from Egypt, Turkey, Spain, Oman and Jerusalem to prestigious builders, architects and wholesalers across the Far East, North America, Europe and the Middle East.

Quality Assurance

AMCO Marble considers quality its most important asset. Our quality control process starts with sourcing the finest raw materials and ends only after the safe arrival of products to our customers.

All slabs and tiles are available polished, honed, brushed, antiqued and sandblasted — each maintained to the highest international standards.
